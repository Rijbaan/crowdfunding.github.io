Our International Community

Tapping into our community starts with activating your own. Most successful projects build a movement, winning over friends and work colleagues as well as general supporters who then share the idea with their vast networks on various social media platforms, and signal their support to the wider range of Cheerful Crowd community. Over 250,000 people, from every different continent on this earth, has backed and funded Cheerful Crowd projects.

Our Mission

: Cheerful Crowd has one mission in life, which is to help bring international investors and backers to SE Asia to bring various projects to life. Our mission is to help underprivileged, uneducated and unfortunate people across SE Asia who simply can not use or know how to go forward due to the poor up bringing they may have accrued. With Cheerful Crowd platform, we want to reach out to all the people who are struggling to find a miracle as banks have turned them down for multiple reasons. Hence why Cheerful Crowd has been created, we don’t judge backgrounds, we help find a solution to their problem, which will there after help them be successful in life to provide for their families. We believe that food and beverage business are essential for everyday life, which has a big potential for creating better future for all parties involved.


We want to help creative people, even those who have never made anything before in their life to take the wheel and be successful. We help business and founders connect directly with their communities, putting power where it belongs for a bright future

Our Team Members

We are an independent company of about 50+ multi cultured people working together. We spend our time designing and building Cheerful Crowd, forging various community around creative and exciting projects, and supporting the ecosystem around us. We are developers, designers, support specialists, writers, musicians, painters, poets, construction consultants and builders, you name it, we have it. Over the last 5 years, our original small team of 8-12 investors has backed more than 165 projects across Thailand currently.
